Dental clinic The Dent

The Dent is a modern wide-ranging dental clinic on Sofiivska Borshchagivka, which combines innovative technologies, a professional team and a comprehensive approach to dental treatment. Our doctors will provide you with high-quality consultation and answer all questions regarding dental treatment.

Welcome to our dental clinic!

We are a team of highly qualified doctors working to ensure your health and comfort. Due to the understanding of how important it is to have healthy teeth and a bright smile, we offer a wide range of services for the whole family.

  • Individual approach
  • Professionalism of doctors
  • Comfort and safety
  • Aesthetic and modern solutions
Interesting fact!
Good oral hygiene not only ensures the health of your mouth, but also contributes to the overall health of the body. Preventing gum and tooth disease can reduce your risk of heart problems and other serious illnesses.
